MORIKAWA MIHO

Morikawa Miho 「森川美穂」 is a Japanese pop singer, born on May 5, 1968 in Osaka. Miho composed several songs for anime, including "Blue Water" and "Yes, I Will" by Fushigi no Umi no Nadia and "Positive" by Ranma 1/2.
