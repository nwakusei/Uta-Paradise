UTADA HIKARU

Utada Hikaru 「宇多田ヒカル」 is a Japanese American pop singer, born on January 19, 1983 in New York, USA. Hikaru became known worldwide after composing songs that are part of the soundtrack of the game franchise "Kingdom Hearts".
