UTA PARADISE DONATE

The function of Uta Paradise is to provide a complete environment that connects all fans of Japanese pop culture to the J-Music universe. All songs are available for download free of charge, as well as other information. In order to make the site more dynamic, interesting, and with a lot of content it is necessary to make available a very relevant time to that.

If you like our work, we ask that (if possible) make a donation of any amount so that the site is maintained and becomes even more a reference for Japanese music in the world. Below you will see a Paypal button to make the donation, and depending on the amount donated it will be possible to place an order so that we can insert a singer or band on our website.
